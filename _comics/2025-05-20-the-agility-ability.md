It’s here we discover what Joe may be experiencing as a singular grace given by God. One of the identifying features of Christ’s resurrected body was his ability to move effortlessly between place and time. St. Thomas Aquinas suggests that this is one of the qualities of ours as well in the resurrection of the dead. Joe seems to have been given this gift while still on this side of eternity.
